About Me

Aditya has always been keen on maintaining an active and fit lifestyle, a trait that is evident from his involvement in various sports and physical activities. He started playing tennis at a young age, even competing in under-12 national qualifiers, which laid the foundation for his love for sports. This passion extended beyond the tennis courts as he trained in MMA for four years at Negi's Academy in Delhi, showcasing his dedication to fitness and discipline. .

Education Background

Before embarking on his college journey at IIIT, Aditya's educational path was distinguished by his role at Decathlon Noida as a sales representative. This experience, which took place before his college years, provided him with valuable insights into the sports industry and honed his skills in customer service and sales. His time at Decathlon, coupled with his academic pursuits, has prepared him for the rigorous and dynamic environment of IIIT.

Achievements
